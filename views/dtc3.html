Major Project #3: Mental Movie

Project Parameters

The requirements for this project was to use Adobe Premier Pro to create a movie that portrays the emotions of a day in your life. For this movie, I focused on a day I had a 6 hour layover at the SeaTac airport. To create this movie, I planned out the idea and found videos to show the emotions of this day.

Technical Aspects

To create this image, I used Adobe Premier Pro. I employed many tools in the creation of this video, including cuts and transitions, audio leveling, and wokring with the clip speed.
